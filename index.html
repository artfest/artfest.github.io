THE FESTIVAL

The Aitchison College Art Society welcomes you to the fifth edition of the most anticipated event of our school, the Art Fest. What started as a small-scale inter-collegiate art competition has established itself as the archetype for national art events to follow.

THE COLLEGE

Aitchison College, Lahore, is one of the most prestigious educational institutions of its kind in South Asia, maintaining the public school tradition of providing an education that uses academics, sports and co-curricular activities as tools for character development.

THE CITY

With a rich history dating back over a millennium, Lahore is often called the 'Heart of Punjab'. Having been the capital of numerous regimes over the centuries, Lahore enjoys a unique positon as the cultural and economic epicentre of the region.
