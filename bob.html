BATTLE OF THE BANDS

.



Rules:
• Each delegation may send in only one team with a maximum of 7 members and a minimum of 3 members.
• Each band will be given a total time of 10 minutes maximum including the sound check and the performance of one song.
• Only the microphone, mic stands, mixer and a standard drum kit will be provided. The rest is to be brought by the bands.
• No session players are allowed (Band members who are not students of a particular delegation).
• It is advised that contestants refrain from portraying high degrees of violence, profanity, drug reference and any other offensive material.

• There will be two rounds, in the first round all the teams will perform and the top bands will be short listed to perform in the final round which will be during the musical night

Points
1st = 5
2nd= 3
3rd= 2
