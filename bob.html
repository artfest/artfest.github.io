BATTLE OF THE BANDS

.



“Music is the universal language of mankind.”-Henry Wadsworth Longfellow
The ultimate of all music battles. Can you feel it in your bones? Can you make us hit the floor? Can you compete with the best bands in the city?

 

Rules:
• Each delegation may send in only one band with a maximum of 7 members and a minimum of 3 members.
• Each band will be given a total time of 7 minutes including the sound check
and the performance of one song.
• Only the microphone, mic stands, mixer and a standard drum kit will
be provided. The rest is to be brought by the bands.
• No session players are allowed (band members who are not students of a
particular delegation).
• Portrayal high degrees of violence, profanity, drug reference and any other offensive material is not allowed.
• There will be two rounds. In the first round all the teams will perform and
the top bands will be short listed. The top teams would perform in the
final round, at the musical evening.


Points
1st = 5
2nd= 3
3rd = 2
