POSTER DESIGN

.


The topics for Poster Designing are
• Music Label Advert (Junior Category)
• Superhero Movie Adverts (Senior Category)

Rules:
• Each delegation may send in two participants, one for the Senior Category and one for the junior Category.
• The time for the Poster Designing Competition is 4 hours for both categories. (Le. juniors and senior)
• Make full use of the size and shape of the paper provided to you. (Approx 15"x 20").
• The poster that the participants make can be 3D.
• Participants must bring in their own an materials. Use materials that will dry quickly. (i.e. pencils, pastels, watercolours etc.).
• Participants will be provided with a reference (such as a phrase or an image)related to the topic, which is to be utilised in their posters.
• The drawings you make should not depict violence, profanity or any other offensive material.

Judging will be on the basis Of:
1) Originality
2) Drawing / Form
3) Overall Design
4) Content
5) Colour / Tone

Points
1st = 3
2nd= 2
3rd= 1
