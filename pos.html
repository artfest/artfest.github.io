DESIGN

“Creativity is allowing yourself to make mistakes. Art is knowing which ones to keep.”-Scott Adams


When you look at a good poster you keep on looking at it, for the purpose of a poster is to look vibrant, alive and yet informative.

 

The topics for Designing are:
• A repaeat pattern tile influenced by the Mughal Era (Junior Category)
• Poster Design; Preserving Heritage (Senior Category)

 

Rules:
• Each delegation may send in two participants, one for the Senior Category and one for the Junior Category.
• The time for the Design Competition is 4 hours for both the
categories. (i.e. Juniors and Seniors)
• The poster for the Senior Category should make full use of the size and
shape of the paper provided. (Approx. 15”x 20”).
• The size of the tile for the Junior Category should be 8” x 8”.
• The poster that the participants make can be 3D.
• Participants must bring in their own art materials. Use materials that will
dry quickly e.g. pencils, pastels, watercolours etc.
• The drawings you make should not depict violence, profanity or any
other offensive material.

 

The Poster will be judged on the basis of:
1) Originality
2) Drawing/Form
3) Overall Design
4) Content
5) Colour/Tone


Points
1st = 3
2nd= 2
3rd= 1
