STILL LIFE

.


The topics for Still Life are
• Metallic Objects (Junior Category)
• Drapery (Senior Category)

Rules:
• Each delegation may send in two participants, one for the Senior Category and one for the junior Category.
• Students must bring their own objects for setting their still life.
• The time for the Still Life Competition is 4 hours for both the categories.(i.e. juniors and seniors)
• Make full use of the size and shape of the paper provided to you.(Approx 15” x 20")
• Participants have the freedom to utilise all sorts of medium. The medium used should dry quickly. e.g. watercolours, pencils, pastels and acrylics.
• The use of rulers and other mechanical means will not be allowed.
• No reference materials, such as books, printouts or photocopies are allowed.
• Participants must bring their own art materials.
• Background and foreground is expected to give substance to the still life.

Judging will be on the basis Of:
1) Composition of Still life on paper
2) Use of medium
5) Drawing/Form
4) Personal Qualities

Points
1st = 3
2nd= 2
3rd= 1
