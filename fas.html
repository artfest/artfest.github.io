FASHION ILLUSTRATION

.





The topics for Fashion Illustration are
• Animal Influence (Junior Category)
• Mythological (Senior Category)

Rules:
• Each delegation may send two teams, one for the Senior Category and one for the junior Category.
• There can be a minimum of 2 and maximum of 4 members in each team.
• The time for the Fashion Illustration category is 2 hours which would include the presentation only.
• Participants are required to bring at least 3 illustrations on the given theme.
• Each team will be provided with a board of 4ft x 4ft on which they will display their illustrations for their line of clothing.
• Participants can display photos, images and even quotations and extracts of writing that have inspired the outfit.
• Participants may bring their designed fabrics or their designs printed on any material.
• Participants have the freedom to utilise any medium
• The illustrations should not depict violence, profanity, drug reference or any other offensive material.
• Participants have to bring all of the material they require for the presentation. Only glues and tapes are to be used to stick the designs on the board (i.e. UHU glue, double tape).

Judging will be on the basis Of:
1) Style
2) Drawing / Form
3) Overall Design
4) Content
5) Colour / Tone

Points
1st = 3
2nd= 2
3rd= 1
