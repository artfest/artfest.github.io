FILM MAKING

.


The topics for Film Making is
• Trailer Making: Humour (Juniors Category)
• Music Video (Seniors Category)

Rules:
• The film must be a maximum of 5 minutes including credits.
• There can be a minimum of 2 and maximum of 5 members in each team.
• All footage must be original and should not have been used in any previouscompetitions. Any video, which does not abide by this rule, will be DISQUALIFIED.
• It is advised that contestants refrain from portraying violence, profanity and any other offensive material.
• Videos must be submitted to The Art Department, Senior School, Aitchison College, Lahore, Shahrah e Quaid-e-Azam, Lahore by 20th October 2016.
• There will be two rounds. In the first round the best films will be short listed. The second round will decide the winners.
Judging will be on the basis Of:
1) Story
2) Originality
3) Directing
4) Editing
Points
1st = 6
2nd= 4
3rd= 3
