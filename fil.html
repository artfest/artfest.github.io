FILM MAKING

.


"We love films and storytelling as a people. it's just a human compulsion to listen to and tell stories"-Mychael Danna


Films allow you to tell stories from your perspective. Everything about making a film distracts you from that first rapturous vision you have of the film and

that’s why you need to get creative when the going gets tough! Just remember, the cameras is your canvas and the world is your medium!

 

The topics for film making are:
• Music Video; Acoustic Music (Junior Category) - [[Example]]
• Trailer Making: Humour(Senior Category)

Explanation for Junior Category: Acoustic music can be downloaded from the internet and used for the music video (footage has to be original and not copied). Delegates are not expected to make their own acoustic music for the videos.

 

Rules:
• Each delegation may send in two teams, one from the Senior Category and
one from the Junior Category.
• The film must be a maximum of 5 minutes.
• There can be a maximum of 7 members in a team.
• All footage must be original and should not have been used in any previous
competitions. Any video that does not abide by this rule will be
DISQUALIFIED.
• Portrayal of violence, profanity and any other offensive material is not allowed.
• Videos must be submitted to: The Art Department, Senior School, Aitchison
College, Shahrah-e-Quaid-e-Azam, Lahore by 8th November 2016, in a USB. Contact Ahmad Saeed: +92 322 3779962
• There will be two rounds. In the first round the best films will be short
listed.
• The winner will be selected from the second round; comprising of
short-listed films from the previous round.

 

The video will be judged on the basis of:
1) Story
2) Originality
3) Cinematography
4) Editing


Points
