INSTRUMENTAL










Rules:
• Each delegation may send in only two participants.
• Each participant will be given a maximum time of 5 minutes including the time of plugging their instruments and performing.
• Only the microphones, mic stands, mixer and a standard sound system will be provided. The rest is to be brought by the participant.
• It is advised that contestants are to refrain from portraying high degrees of violence, profanity, drug reference and any other offensive material.
• There will be two rounds, in the first round all the teams will perform and the top contestants will be short listed for the final round.

Points
1st = 3
2nd= 2
3rd= 1
