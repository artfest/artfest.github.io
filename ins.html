INSTRUMENTAL










“Where words fail, music speaks.”-Hans Christian Andersen
Unchained and in liberty, not limited to words, instruments are timeless and the music they make is eternal. So can you express yourself without words?

 

Rules:
• Each delegation may send in only 2 participants.
• Each participant will be given a maximum time of 5 minutes including the
time for plugging their instruments and performing.
• Only the microphones, mic stands, mixer and a standard sound system
will be provided. The rest is to be brought by the participant.
• Participants are to refrain from portraying high degrees of violence,
profanity, drug reference and any other offensive material.
• There will be two rounds. In the first round all the teams will perform and
the top contestants will be short listed for the final round.


Points
1st = 3
2nd= 2
3rd= 1
