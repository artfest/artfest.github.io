PHOTOGRAPHY

.


The topics for Photography are
• Old is Gold - Old Objects (Junior Category)
• Silver Lining - Landscapes (Senior Category) Rules: • Each delegation may send in two participants, one for the Senior Categoryand one for the junior Category.
• There can be a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 5 members in each team
• There will be two rounds of photography competition.
• In round 1 each team will be provided with a board of 4x4 feet on which the display of photographs can be designed.
• The top 5 teams from both categories will continue to the second round.
• In round 2, contestants will be required to take photographs on campus in allotted areas.
• Teams will have to submit their photographs in a USB before the end of the second day. The printing in Round 2 will be taken care of by the ArtFest team, for which contestants will be charged.
• They will then decorate the board and the winners will be decided based on these displays.
• Each team will have to display 5 to 10 photographs.
• Pictures should be unedited and not plagiarised.
• Participants must bring in their own materials for presentation.
• Only glue or tape may be used to stick pictures to the board.

Judging will be on the basis Of:
1) Content
2) Originality
3) Quality of images
4) Use of Light
5)Display on the board

Points
1st = 5
2nd= 3
3rd= 2
