The story for the news break will be given on the first day of the event.

Rules:
• Each delegation may send in one team of minimum 5 and maximum 7 members
• The news break can be a minimum of 1 and a half minute to a maximum of 5 minutes
• Before the start of the event, the contestants will be given the story with all the details. Contestants are required to shoot a news segment deciding how to break the story on the imaginary news channel
• Contestants can use any person present at the event for acting.
• All footage must be original and should not be used in any other competition. Any video that does not abide by this rule will be disqualified.
• It is advised that contestants refrain from portraying high degrees of violence, profanity and any other offensive material.
• Videos must be in AVI, WMV or MP4 format.
• Videos must be submitted an hour before the screening to the head of event.
• All graphics used must be original.

Judging will be on the basis Of:
1) Originality
2) Story
3) Cinematography
4) Editing

Points
1st = 5
2nd= 4
3rd= 3
