THEMATIC ART

.


The topics for Thematic art are
• Halloween (Junior Category)
• Surrealism (Senior Category)

Rules:
• Each delegation may send in two participants, one for the Senior Category and one for the junior Category.
• The time for the Thematic Art competition is 4 hours for both the categories. (i.e. juniors and seniors)
• Make full use of the size and shape of the paper provided to you.
(Approx. 15"x 20")
• Participants have the freedom to utilize all sorts of mediums, however, the medium used should dry quickly e.g. watercolors, pastels, pencils, acrylics.
• The use of rulers and other mechanical instruments will not be allowed.
• Reference materials, such as books, printouts or photocopies are allowed.
• Participants must bring their own art material and drawing boards.
• The drawings you make must not depict violence, profanity, drug reference or any other offensive material.

Judging will be on the basis Of:
1) Originality
2) Drawing/Form
3) Color/Tone
4) Personal Qualities

Points
1st = 3
2nd= 2
3rd= 1
