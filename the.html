THEMATIC ART

.

“Art is not what you see, but what you make others see.”-Edgar Deges


The reason Artists live a long life is, for them, every painting proves to be a new adventure. They’re always looking ahead to something new and exciting.

 

The topics for Thematic Art are:
• Halloween (Junior Category)
• Surrealism (Senior Category)

 

Rules:
• Each delegation may send in two participants, one for the Senior Category and one for the Junior Category.
• The time for the Thematic Art competition is 4 hours for both the categories. (i.e. Juniors and Seniors)
• Make full use of the size and shape of the paper provided to you. (Approx. 15”x 20”)
• Participants have the freedom to utilise all sorts of mediums, however, the medium used should dry quickly e.g. watercolours, pastels, pencils, acrylics.
• The use of rulers and other mechanical instruments will not be allowed.
• Reference materials, such as books, printouts or photocopies are allowed.
• Participants must bring their own art material and drawing boards.
• The artwork must not depict violence, profanity, drug reference or any other offensive material.

 

Thematic Art will be judged on the basis of:
1) Originality
2) Drawing/Form
3) Colour/Tone
4) Personal Qualities

 

Points
1st = 3
2nd= 2
3rd= 1
