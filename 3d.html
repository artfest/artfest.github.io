3D ART

.


The topic for 3D Art is
• Abstract

Rules:
• Each delegation may send in one team of minimum 2 and maximum 4 members to work on a single sculpture.
• The time for the 3D Art category is 6 hours with one hour of break in between.
• References are allowed.
• Participants have the freedom to use any medium for their sculpture. e.g. clay, junk, wire, etc.
• Participants must bring all the art materials required.
• The sculpture should not depict any profanity, drug reference or any other offensive material.
• There is no restriction of size for the sculpture.
• A carpenter will be available in the premises

Judging will be on the basis Of:
1) Form/Shape
2) Use of Medium
3) Originality
4) Quality of Work & Finesse

Points
1st = 5
2nd= 3
3rd= 2
