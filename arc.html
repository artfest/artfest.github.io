ARCHITECTURAL DRAWING

.


The topic for Architectural Drawing is
• Gurdwara

Rules:
• The time for the Architectural Drawing competition is 6 hours with an hour break in between.
• Make full use of the size and shape of the paper provided to you.(Approx. 15"x 20")
• Drawing must be mono chromatic.
• Participants have the freedom to utilise all sorts of mediums e.g. pencil, charcoal, dry pastel etc.
• Participants must bring all the art materials required. (drawing boards and mediums etc.)
• The use of rulers and other mechanical instruments will not be allowed.
• Background and foreground is expected to give substance to your drawing.
Judging will be on the basis Of:
1) Drawing/Form
2)Composition
3)Personal Qualities
4)Closeness to Readily

Points
1st = 3
2nd= 2
3rd= 1
