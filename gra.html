DIGITAL ART

.


The topics for Digital Art are
A logo, poster, and billboard design for a mobile app
Rules:
• Each delegation may send a maximum of two participants
• Students will provided with a band name, at the start of the competition
• Delegates may use any graphic designing software
• Use of any sort of predesigned template will lead to disqualification.
• Use of hardware such as drawing pads e.g. Bamboo Pads, iPads or tablets is permissible.
• The ArtFest team will provide Internet access. Delegates may use references, small vector images and/or use images taken by their delegation in regard to the Photography competition.
• The illustrations should not depict violence, profanity, drug reference or any other offensive material.
• Teams will have to submit their art work in USBs by the end of the allotted time. The ArtFest team will be responsible for the printing of the submissions
Judging will be on the basis Of
1) Relevance of the artwork to the given themes and topics
2) Skills and design sense
3) Innovation and Creativity.
Points
1st = 3
2nd= 2
3rd= 1
