“A work of art is above all an adventure of the mind.”-Eugene Ionesco
What are the principles for a good design to be effective? These are questions that many designers are seeking answers for.

If you think you have what it takes then come down to the Art & Performing Arts Fest and show the designers what they really need to see.

 

The topic of Digital Art is:

• A Music Festival


Rules:
• Each delegation may send in a maximum of two participants.
• Participants will be provided with the name and theme (traditional, jazz etc.) of
the music festival at the start of the competition.
• The time for the Digital Art Competition is 6 hours, with an hour’s break in between.
• Participants may use any graphic designing software however the
Art Fest Management suggests the use of Adobe Photoshop.
• Use of any sort of pre-designed template will lead to disqualification.
• Use of hardware such as drawing pads e.g. Bamboo Pads, iPads or tablets
is permissible.
• Delegates will be given access to the Internet. They may use
references, small vector images and/or use images taken by their delegation
in the Photography competition.
• Participants will be required to produce a logo for the event alongside a promotional
poster (11” x 16”).
• Teams will have to submit their artwork in USBs by the end of the allotted time.
Printing will be taken care of by the Art Fest team, for which contestants will be
charged.

 

The submissions will be judged on the following criteria:
1) Relevance of the artwork to the given themes and topics.
2) Skills shown in the artwork related to digital art and
graphic designing.
3) Innovation and
Creativity.

 

Points
1st = 3
2nd= 2
3rd= 1
