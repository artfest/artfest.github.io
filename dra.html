DRAMATICS




Rules:
• Each delegation may send teams for this event
• Teams must perform a skit of 10 to 15 minutes long
• Each team shall comprise of 8 to 12 members (including a sound coordinator, a light coordinator and two backstage coordinators)
• The team must present their slit to the dramatics team on the 9th November, 2016. Teams will be informed about the time through an e-mail or a phone call.
• The slit must not have explicit material; religious/political jokes and plagiarism will not be tolerated.
• Teams need to bring their own props except tables, chairs etc.
• There will be only one round. All teams shall present their skits to a panel of judges in the Amphitheatre on Friday me 20th of February 2015.
Judging will be on the basis Of:
1) Originality
2) Script
3) Expressions
4) Dialogue Delivery
Points
1st = 7
2nd= 5
3rd= 3
