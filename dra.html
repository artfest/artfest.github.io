DRAMATICS

.


“Movies will make you famous; Television will make you rich; But theatre will make you good.”-Terrence Mann
Can you create a character, and breathe into it perpetual reality and life? Can you make it co-exist yet be in conflict with other characters?

If you can, that means you understand Dramatics.


Rules:
• Each delegation may send in one team for this category.
• Teams must perform a skit 8 to 10 minutes long.
• Each team shall comprise of 8 to 12 members (including a sound
coordinator, a light coordinator and two backstage coordinators).
• The team must present their skit to the dramatics team on the 9th &
10th November, 2016. Teams will be informed about the time through an e-mail
or a phone call.
• The skit must not have explicit material; religious/political jokes and
plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
• Teams need to bring their own props except tables, chairs etc.
• There will be only one round. All teams shall present their skits to a panel
of judges in the Amphitheatre on Friday the 11th of November 2016.

 

The drama will be judged on the basis of:
1) Originality
2) Script
3) Expressions
4) Dialogue Delivery
