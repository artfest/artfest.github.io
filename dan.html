DANCE

.


The topic for Dance is>
• Fusion

Rules:
• Each delegation can send in only one team.
• Each team must consist of 3 to 8 members.
• Lights/sound manager may accompany the team and must be registered as well.
• Sound and light arrangements shall be made by the ArtFest Management.
• The dancers may or may not be accompanied by a makeup artist. The makeup artist however must be registered as well.
• The makeup artist may serve as props manager as well.
• Retakes will only be allowed due to a technical fault on the managements behalf.
• Participants must bring their props on the days of the events and will be responsible for them.
• Teams need to bring their own props. The ArtFest Management shall provide props such as chairs and benches etc.
• Performances may last from 3-7 minutes.
• Music/backing track tiles need to be submitted to the Category Head a day before the event on USB, in Mp3 or AAC format only.
• Teams may rehearse on the 9th & 10th November, 2016. They will be informed about the time of their performance through an e-mail or a phone call.
• The dance must not have explicit material; religious offence and plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
• There will be only one round. All teams shall present their dance to a panel of judges in the Amphitheatre.
• No lights or sounds will be provided on the day of rehersals (9th & 10th November)

Points
1st = 5
2nd= 4
3rd= 3
