VOCALS

.


Rules:
• Each delegation may send in only four participants. An instrumentalist may accompany the singers but after his/her registration.
• A maximum of two instruments will be allowed.
• Karaoke songs are allowed. However the participants must ensure no background vocals are present.
• Each participant will be given a maximum of 5 minutes including the sound check and the performance of one song.
• Medleys are allowed however the team can choose a category once (Eastern or Western) and after that it will not be changed.
• Only the microphones, mic stands, mixer and a standard sound system will be provided. The rest is to be brought by me participant.
• It is advised that contestants refrain from portraying high degrees of violence, profanity, drug reference and any other offensive material.
• There will be three rounds, in the first round all the teams will perform a song, followed by a second round and the top contestants will be soon listed for the final round at the musical evening.

The Awards will be:
1) Best Eastern Singer
2) Best Western Singer
Points
1st = 3
2nd= 2
3rd= 1
