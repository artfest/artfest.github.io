VOCALS

.

“Music is the strongest form of magic.”-Marilyn Manson
To sing, to mesmerise, to petrify, to let your emotions flow is singing. So do not hold back.

 

The sub-categories for Vocals are:
• Eastern
• Western

 

Rules:
• Each delegation may send in 4 participants, a maximum of 2 from each
category. An instrumentalist may accompany the singers but after
his/her registration.
• A maximum of two instruments will be allowed.
• Karaoke songs are allowed. However the participants must ensure no
background vocals are present.
• Each participant will be given a maximum of 5 minutes including the sound
check and the performance of one song.
• The participant can choose a category once (Eastern or Western) and after
that it may not be changed.
• Only the microphones, mic stands, mixer and a standard sound system
will be provided. The rest is to be brought by the participant.
• Participants are not allowed to portray high degrees of violence,
profanity, drug reference and any other offensive material.
• Medleys are allowed, however eastern medleys can only have eastern songs
and western ones can only have western songs.
• There will be three rounds. In the first round, all the teams will perform
a song, followed by a second round. The top contestants will be short
listed for the final round at the musical evening.
• The results of the first round will be posted on the Art & Performing Arts
Fest facebook page at the end of the first day.

 

The Awards will be:
1) Best Eastern Singer
2) Best Western Singer

 

Points
1st = 3
2nd= 2
3rd= 1
