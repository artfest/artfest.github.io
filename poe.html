POETRY

.


I would define, in breif, the poetry of words as the rhythmical creation of beauty. -Edgar Allan Poe

 

Rules:
• Each delegation can send a maximum of two participants.
• Participants must hand in their poems in print on the first day (11th November 2016) to the category head.
• Participants are also allowed to perform their own poems.
• The first round will take place on the second day of the competition (12th November 2016) in the Babar Ali Library
• The short listed participants will then proceed to the final round on the third day of the competition (13th November 2016) where they must recite another poem in front of an audience.
• The recitation should be a minimum of 1.5 minute and maximum of 3.5 minutes
• The poet may not use props, costumes or musical instruments
• Participants are not allowed to portray high degrees of violence, profanity, drug reference and any other offensive material.

 

Points
1st = 3
2nd= 2
3rd= 1
